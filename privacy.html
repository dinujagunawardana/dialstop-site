Dial Stop Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Dial Stop is a casual arcade game created by Dinuja Gunawardana. This policy explains what information may be collected when you use the app.

Information Stored by the App

Dial Stop stores gameplay information such as coins and high score locally on your device. The app does not require an account.

Advertising

Dial Stop uses Google AdMob to show rewarded ads and interstitial ads. Google AdMob may collect information such as device identifiers, advertising data, app interaction data, diagnostics, performance data, and approximate location derived from network information for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention.

Learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services at Google's partner sites policy.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob and related Google advertising services. These services are governed by Google's privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Dial Stop is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can help address it.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact dialstopsupport@gmail.com.
